Project Introduction

HUT: Enabling High-UTility, Batched Queries under Differential Privacy Protection for Internet-of-Vehicles

Designed, developed and optimized HUT, a Differential Privacy (DP) enabled algorithm for high data utility and privacy protection in the context of Internet-of-Vehicles (IoV), by leveraging local batch aggregation and order constraints. Evaluated HUT with state-of-the-art algorithms, which achieves 96.9% reduction in information loss while maintaining strong mathematically-guaranteed privacy protection.

Motivation

Motivated by the unbalanced data utility and privacy protection in applying DP for IoV, Oneiros aims to:
(1) Balance the tradeofd between data utility and privacy protection.
(2) Address the unique issue of IoV query: query sequences are usually formed as frequent and unbalanced batches in practices.
