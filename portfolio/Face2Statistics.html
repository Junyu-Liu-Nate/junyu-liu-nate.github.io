Project Intoduction

Face2Statistics: User-Friendly, Low-Cost and Effective Alternative to In-Vehicle Sensors/Monitors for Drivers

Designed and developed Face2Statistics, a comprehensive roadmap to deliver user-friendly, low-cost and effective alternatives for extracting drivers’ statistics and contextual data from facial images. By leverage HSV color space and Conditional Random Field, the robustness in terms of prediction accuracy and degree of customization are improved. Our evaluations show that, Face2Statistics can be effective alternatives to sensors/monitors for Heart Rate, Skin Conductivity and Vehicle Speed.
