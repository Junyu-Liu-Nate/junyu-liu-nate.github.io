My Research Journey

I began with several Human-Computer Interaction works, focusing on (semi-)autonomous vehicles, with an exploration in interactive toolkit designs, privacy protection techniques, and multi-modal predictions of statistics.

I pusue my love of Computer Graphics and dig deeper into Physics-based character animation, Geometry Processing, and Simulation. Now, my main interest is Geometry Processing and Digital Fabrication.
