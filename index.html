About Me

I am currently a Y4 UG student at University of Nottingham Ningbo China, majoring in Computer Science.

Currently, I am collaborating with Prof. Daniele Panozzo at New York University and Prof. Teseo Schneider at University of Victoria.

My research interests lie in the Computer Graphics and Human-Computer-Interaction fields. My work on Computer Graphics center on geometry processing and simulation. I also lead a series of works on physics-based character control and animation, intersecting with machine learning. For Human-Computer-Interaction, my research focuses on (semi-)autonomous vehicles, with an exploration in interactive toolkit designs, privacy protection techniques, and multi-modal predictions of statistics.
